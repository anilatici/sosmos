🌍 Authentic Turkish Street Food

Made with Passion, Rooted in Tradition

Sosmos brings the heart of Turkish street food to Melbourne with recipes inspired by Istanbul's vibrant food culture. Every roll, wrap, and mussel is prepared fresh with care, balancing tradition with the best local ingredients. We're proud to share food that feels familiar, bold, and full of soul.

What's On the Menu

A few customer favourites from our truck.

Barramundi Roll

Fresh local barramundi, crisp salad, and bold Turkish spice in a soft market-style roll.

Steamed Rice Stuffed Mussels (Midye)
