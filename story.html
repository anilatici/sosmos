Our Story

Home / Our Story

From Istanbul's Streets to Melbourne's Market

Sosmos began with memories of Istanbul's busy streets where food is warm, quick, and full of character. The scent of grilled meats, lemon squeezed over midye, and soft buns soaked in rich flavour inspired us long before the truck opened.

When we brought Sosmos to Queen Victoria Market, our goal was simple: serve food that feels authentic, comforting, and proudly Turkish while celebrating the incredible local produce in Melbourne.

Every day we cook with intention, from the first wrap to the last roll, because we want each customer to taste the same passion and tradition that started this journey.

Real Ingredients. Real Tradition.

🐟 Fresh & Local

We source the freshest local barramundi and quality ingredients for vibrant flavour in every bite.

🫙 Authentic Recipes

Our recipes are rooted in genuine Turkish street food traditions, shaped by generations of home kitchens and markets.

❤️ Made with Care

Every order is prepared fresh to order with care, consistency, and the warmth you'd expect from a family-run truck.

Our Home - Queen Victoria Market

Queen Victoria Market is one of Melbourne's most iconic destinations, known for its diversity, community spirit, and food culture. We're proud to be part of that energy, serving Turkish street food alongside some of the city's most loved local traders.
